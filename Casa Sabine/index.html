Welcome to Casa Sabine

We would like to welcome you in our beautifully appointed luxury villa. Casa Sabine is a modern spacious family home, available directly from the owner or on AirBnB.

It offers stunning views over Telendos, and shady or sunny terraces along with uninterrupted views of the sea and the mountains. We guarantee youa tranquil memorable stay!

Accommodation

Modern, well appointed and spacious; Sleeps 6-9 people depending on group composition; Fast WiFi.

Location

Directly under climbing sectors from Poets to Grande Grotta; 5min from Massouri bars and restaurants; Swimming from secluded beaches nearby.
