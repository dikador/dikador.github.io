    IN RUNING, YOU DECIDE WHAT WINNING MEANS

    The obsession with running is really an obsession with the potential for more and more life.

    — George Sheehan, Organizator

    If you’ve been working out regularly, you’ve already discovered it: No matter how good or bad you feel at any given moment, exercise will make you feel better.
    And it goes beyond just the “runner’s high”—that rush of feel-good hormones known as endocannabinoids.

    FEATURES OF OUR MARATHON

    FINISHER MEDAL

    The adrenaline rush of excitement and joy when the finisher medal falls in your hands could be one of the best moments in your running journey.

    RACE SHIRT

    Fire up your engine and get your custom racing shirt made of the best materials. Racing apparel is an attribute associated with inspiring professional runners.

    BIB NUMBER

    Stand out of the crowd with a unique racing number that will bring you luck and victory.

    RACE PHOTO

    That's right – your digital race photos are free compliments of our Marathon! Be sure to wear your bib number facing front, and SMILE!

    BAG REFRESHMENTS

    Water stations with beverages, fruit and medical personnel will be awaiting you at the start line, finish line and along the track.
